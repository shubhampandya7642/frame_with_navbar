Mukesh Ambani


Mukesh Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman and the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries Limited (RIL). RIL is India's most valuable company by market value. Ambani was born in Aden, Yemen on April 19, 1957. He joined Reliance in 1981 and is the largest shareholder of the company.

Ambani is the eldest son of Dhirubhai Ambani, the founder of Reliance Industries. Ambani's net worth is approximately $24 billion. He owns approximately 45% of Reliance Industries.
