Elon Musk


Elon Musk is an entrepreneur and industrial engineer who is the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX. He is also the founder of The Boring Company, Neuralink, and OpenAI. In 2022, he purchased Twitter for $44 billion.

Musk was born in South Africa and moved to the United States. He co-founded PayPal and founded SpaceX in 2002. He became the CEO of Tesla in 2008.

Musk is known for giving his employees seemingly impossible deadlines. He is also known for running X (formerly Twitter) from his iPhone. Musk's IQ is reported to be 155, which is very high compared to the average of 100.
