Satya Nadella


Satya Nadella is the CEO and executive chairman of Microsoft. He was born in Hyderabad, India on August 19, 1967. He joined Microsoft in 1992 and held various roles before becoming CEO in 2014.

Nadella replaced Steve Ballmer as CEO. Before that, he was the EVP of the cloud and enterprise group. He has led the company away from a failing mobile strategy and focused on other segments, including cloud computing and augmented reality.

Nadella has also overseen major acquisitions. Under his leadership, Microsoft has more cloud computing revenue than Google, more subscribers than Netflix, and a near-trillion-dollar market capital.
