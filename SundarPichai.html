Sundar Pichai


Sundar Pichai is an Indian-born American business executive. He is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google. He was born in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India on June 10, 1972. He joined Google in 2004 and helped develop Google Toolbar and Google Chrome. He has been the CEO of Google since 2015 and the CEO of Alphabet since 2019.

Pichai is an iconic figure in the tech industry. He was born into a modest family in Tamil Nadu, India. He moved away in 1989 to study metallurgical engineering at IIT Kharagpur. He completed a B. Tech at IIT Kharagpur.
